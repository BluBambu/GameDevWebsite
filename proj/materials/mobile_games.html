Popular F2P mobile games

Two key features that are optimized for:

  • Addictiveness - Players will play every day
  • Viralness - Players will share it with their friends
The goal:
Get them to pay you money often.


(See: DAU metric)

Rayman Fiesta Run




The good:
  • Excellent graphics and music (port from a console game)
The bad:
  • 3$ upfront cost
  • Unnecessary IAP feature
  • Annoying, spammy facebook share

Castleville Legends




The good:
  • Good fit for a mobile format (short play sessions)
  • Lots of longevity from a relatively small amount of content
  • Useful IAP boosts
The bad:
  • Lack of variety and interesting content
  • Cannot be played for long play sessions
  • Progression becomes increasingly slow at higher levels

Puzzle & Dragons




The good:
  • Addictive Pokemon-style character progression
  • Appealing (to some) art style
  • Incentives to play daily
  • Easy and rewarding to connect and play with friends
The bad:
  • Core gameplay is repetitive
